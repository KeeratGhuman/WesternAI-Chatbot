Project Associations
     This project is associated with Western AI, A division in Western University that focuses on striving to promote students at Western to learn and grow in the Artificial Intellegence (AI) indusry in various positions. This is specifically involved with Western AI projects, where once a year, students are formed in multiple groups to work on creating AI driven projects that will be later presented in two different showcases. 
     The Western Showcase is held on February 28th 2023, which allows all groups from Western AI projects to present what they have designed over the course of the work term. A selection of judges who have vast experience in the industry to analyze each other projects presented where awards will be distributed as well. The second showcase is the Canadian Undergraduate Conference in Artificial Intelligence (CUCAI). It his held on March 5th 2023 where students across the country who are interested in the AI industry have the opportunity to gather for a weekend to learn more and grow from other experts and present their own projects that they have created.
